Calopsita

Calopsita is a natural bird of Australia, he was once considered a crested parrot or small cockatoo.

  • Scientific Name: Nymphicus hollandicus
  • Average Lenght: 30 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 25 years
  • Habitat: Large areas of arid or semi-arid climate

With gray color(generally), head yellow, the Calopsita is a bird that enchants anyone, because is very friend of humans, your relationship with them is very interesting. The calopsitas have the capacity of decorate sounds and voices.
